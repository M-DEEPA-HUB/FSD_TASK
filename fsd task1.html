⚡ Harry Potter and the Wizarding World ⚡

Harry Potter is one of the most beloved fictional characters of all time. Created by J.K. Rowling, Harry is a young wizard who discovers his magical heritage on his eleventh birthday. His adventures at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry have inspired millions of readers and movie lovers around the world.

🪄 Main Characters

  • ⚡ Harry Potter
  • 🧡 Ron Weasley
  • 📚 Hermione Granger
  • 🦉 Albus Dumbledore
  • 🐍 Lord Voldemort

🏰 Magical Facts About Hogwarts

  1. Students are sorted into four houses.
  2. Owls deliver letters and messages.
  3. Magic is taught through spells and potions.
  4. The castle has moving staircases and secret passages.
  5. The famous sport played is Quidditch.
